2025 Anime Updates: What to Watch This Year

Stay updated on the latest anime releases and trends for 2025

Sakamoto Days

In January 2025, Sakamoto Days is set to captivate audiences with its unique blend of action, humor, and heartwarming moments. Adapted from Yuto Suzuki's hit manga, this anime follows Taro Sakamoto, a former assassin turned grocer, as he navigates his peaceful life while dealing with remnants of his violent past.

Fire Force Season 3

Slated for April 2025, Fire Force Season 3 is highly anticipated by fans. This season dives into the Great Cataclysm arc, exploring the origins of the Infernals and Shinra Kusakabe's mysterious past. With David Production at the helm, expect intense battles and significant character development.

Solo Leveling Season 2

Airing in January 2025, Solo Leveling Season 2 continues the story of Jin-Woo Sung, a Hunter who gains the power to level up. This season promises to up the ante with improved animation, story reveals, and character interactions.

Produced by A-1 Pictures, this anime is expected to be one of the best of 2025, with fans eagerly awaiting its release.
