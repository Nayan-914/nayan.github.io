Number Systems & Binary Mathematics

Number Systems

  • Decimal (Base 10): Everyday numbers (0-9).
  • Binary (Base 2): Used in computers (0, 1).
  • Octal (Base 8): (0-7).
  • Hexadecimal (Base 16): (0-9, A-F).

Conversion Highlights

  • Binary to Decimal: Multiply each bit by 2position and sum.
  • Decimal to Binary: Divide by 2, record remainders in reverse.
  • Binary to Octal: Group bits by 3.
  • Binary to Hexadecimal: Group bits by 4.

Complements

  • 1's Complement: Invert bits (0 becomes 1, 1 becomes 0).
  • 2's Complement: 1's complement + 1. Used for subtraction.
  • 9's & 10's Complement: Used for subtraction in decimal.

Binary Math

  • Addition: 0+0=0, 1+0=1, 0+1=1, 1+1=10.
  • Subtraction: Uses borrowing when necessary.
  • Multiplication: Standard multiplication rules.
